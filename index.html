EBEC Project

EBEC (European BEST Engineering Competition) is an engineering competition, available to European students of technology, that provides its participants with the opportunity to develop their teamwork skills, as well as to test their creativity and versatility, through the solving of a problem of either of the two categories: Case Study and Team Design.

Case Study

It consists of finding a solution to a technology problem by developing a possible strategy to combat the problem addressed, within a period of 24H.

Team Design

It consists of solving, designing, and building, a creative and functional prototype, within a period of 24H and with a limited number of materials available.

EBEC Portugal is the second stage of the international competition, in our country, wherein, the winners of the local rounds (Almada, Aveiro, Coimbra, Lisbon and Porto) are going to be pitted against each-other. The two teams that come out on top will have the chance to represent Portugal abroad, in the competition’s last stage, which is going to take place in Madrid, Spain, in the month of july of 2021. This year, EBEC Portugal’s 12th issue will ensue in “Universidade de Coimbra”, in Coimbra, between 14 and 17 of may.
